## 2. **理解 Shopee Open Platform账户类型**

1. **Individual Seller **- 在没有营业执照的情况下，在 Shopee 上销售的个人帐户。（仅支持服务您的 Shopee 商店。）
1. **Registered Business Seller** - 具有营业执照的情况下，在 Shopee 上销售的企业帐户。（仅支持服务您的 Shopee 商店。）
1. **Third-party Partner Platform (ISV)** - 在Shopee平台上，向卖家提供服务的具有营业执照的企业帐户。


**您可以使用下面的账户类型选择表来查看您应该选择哪种账户类型，请注意：**

- 您可以在虾皮开放平台上创建的 APP ，取决于您申请的账户类型，您可以查看 <u>App management page</u>了解详情.
- 此外，您需要根据您经营的市场和您要申请的账户类型，来满足Shopee Open Platform资格标准。
| **                                                                                                                Account Type Selection** |
| --- |
| **Are you a Shopee seller?** | **Are you an individual or an enterprise?** | **Which Account Type should you choose?** | **Description** |
| Yes | Individual | **Shopee Seller > Individual Seller** | Accounts of individuals who sell on Shopee without a business license.*(Note: You can only provide software services to your own Shopee shop.)* |
| Yes | Enterprise | **Shopee Seller > Registered Business Seller** | Accounts of enterprises selling on Shopee with a registered license.*(Note: You can only provide software services to your own Shopee shop.)* |
| No | **Third-party Partner Platform (ISV)** | Accounts of enterprises with registered licenses providing services to sellers on Shopee. |

### **3.2. Third-party Partner Platforms**


如果您要入驻 Shopee Open Platform, 您必须满足以下条件：


1. You’re a registered business. Provide valid business documents during your application.
1. Your business product has to be live, with existing ecommerce integrations which are identifiable via a provided trial account.
1. Your services should not include suspicious activities such as the selling of cross-border accounts, or extracting listing information to enable transactions outside of Shopee. You may refer to our <u>Platform Partner Rules</u> for more information.
1. Submit live versions of (a) your business product’s URL, (b) trial account login details and enable all features for Shopee’s testing purposes.
1. Your live business product URL must use HTTPS (https://), support TLS 1.2 or higher, and maintain a security rating of “A”


如果您是在下述地区注册的开发者，请注意适用于您的资质要求：**TW (Taiwan)**

1. Your existing ecommerce integration(s) must be with a major ecommerce platform that has regulations for user data privacy. 


**CB-CN (Cross Border-China)**

1. The business scope of your business license must be software development.
1. Your registered capital must be higher than 100,000 RMB.
1. You should have notified your Account Manager regarding your application.


**CB-KR (Cross Border-Korea), CB-JP (Cross Border-Japan)**

1. You should have notified your Account Manager regarding your application.
## 4.提交开发者入驻资质信息以供审核
### 4.1 登陆Shopee Open Platform > Console > **App Management** > **App List** >** **根据业务资料的描述信息选择更适合您的帐户类型


*⚠️注意：请参阅理解 Shopee Open Platform帐户类型部分，以了解更多账号资质信息。*


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=EwPS%2FCa%2BriT%2FKQ8BOPgIUksLG%2BnHh2MXxeaINlfVbkwII05GYG9uXOUBespPdUy7uXyzTF9vvINVbFBY3R%2F7%2Fg%3D%3D&image_type=png)


* Shopee Open Platform Console：指您管理所有 Shopee Open Platform 事务的站点。

### 4.2 根据您的账户类型填写详细信息


**A. Shopee Seller > Individual Seller**


**B. Shopee Seller > Registered Business Seller**


**C. Third-party Partner Platform**

#### **4.2.1 Shopee Seller **>** Individual Seller**


i) 选择 Shopee Seller 作为您的账户类型后，您将被重定向以验证您的 Shopee Seller 账户，填写正确的 Shopee 卖家账户凭据 > 选择登录以完成验证。


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=%2FnEci3Chf9eOXfEkaogV3FUlxbe4UEc6dt3%2Fx3DG3snyIG01ss2dU4GNHzeK83f%2BEmYsQC3XzeTZMf92nIhrEw%3D%3D&image_type=png)



ii) 如果您的卖家账户验证成功，您可以继续填写Shopee Seller其他身份信息。



从Seller Type下拉菜单中选择**Individual Seller** > 填写您的**大客户经理邮箱(Key Account Manager's Email)** > 选择**下一步**。



⚠️*注意：大客户经理的邮箱应该是有效的 Shopee邮箱（以@shopee.com 结尾），如果没有大客户经理邮箱，可以不填。*


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=UtQrrX4Xj5dp6RyChxF5M1rZxfQ7eC55S7X%2FNGgtXF5ZCKslLQNLyOfpQ69Qli0qc%2Fq4muF0LW3D6MNcc9X11w%3D%3D&image_type=png)



iii) 填写所有个人资料详细信息 > 选择提交。


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=c0xRhMxRvhRgicYKr%2Fg5q5CGsKqJ1jf%2BxEBh185uh8%2BcJLl%2BDDWEC%2BwIJBVDMt2wlz%2F1JD064yAxmzj19l1l9w%3D%3D&image_type=png)

#### **4.2.2 Shopee Seller** > **Registered Business Seller**


i) 选择 Shopee Seller 作为您的帐户类型后，您将被重定向以验证您的 Shopee 卖家帐户 填写正确的 Shopee 卖家帐户凭据 > 选择登录以完成验证。


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=x0u%2Bk5GL6aQ024AsmmEqTC5i7iD8fxxAqtqxOkvDIwM8NNDUE%2BTm5wZl0nBXZcO5UOhZ1bMKYvjoLzthy9PzcA%3D%3D&image_type=png)



ii) 如果您的卖家账户验证成功，您可以继续填写Shopee Seller其他身份信息。



从Seller Type下拉菜单中选择**Registered Business Seller** > 填写您的**大客户经理邮箱(Key Account Manager's Email)** > 选择**下一步**。



⚠️*注意：大客户经理的邮箱应该是有效的 Shopee邮箱（以@shopee.com 结尾），如果没有大客户经理邮箱，可以不填。*


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=m1BdZzJoOLDtJeOsSpje6%2FP%2BtsGUw%2B25la36oO%2FbXTdbj6svIR3tb0vKA6YWydz4R18qed3RaRmRRNlXTEEZ4w%3D%3D&image_type=png)


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=j6emGdURomA3VVMTMGx3ONXdrwyOlNDAtusD3ddT0mFkvG%2FhEati7Zrk7zRRLc2yfEKyo55WCd43XOWt5tK%2BrQ%3D%3D&image_type=png)

#### **4.2.3 Third-party Partner Platform**


i) 选择**Third-party Partner Platform**作为您的帐户类型后，您将被重定向到商业信息部分，填写所有个人资料详细信息 > 选择**下一步**。


![图片](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=1KpELAxgq%2FeEiJRjeUwDmKlI9OUc1jQxa7pp8hv0gpPyAfFz5V3ZyC9vYjVsWvXLoN9fOijCMpJuMtfQYJ87kg%3D%3D&image_type=png)



ii) 在服务信息部分下，填写您的软件服务详细信息并选择提交。



⚠️*注意：请提供您的软件登录地址和线上环境测试帐户，以供 Shopee 验证， URL 应以“https://”开头并使用 TLS 1.2 协议。*


### 4.3 请您耐心等待，您的个人资料将会被 Shopee审核通过


一旦您的申请获得通过，您将通过您提交的电子邮件地址收到一封通知邮件。



*⚠️ *注意：***Shopee Seller***申请将在 3 个工作日内处理，而**Third-party Partner Platform**申请将在 10 个工作日内处理。
