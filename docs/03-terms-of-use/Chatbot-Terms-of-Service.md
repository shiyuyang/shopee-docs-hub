### **1. Purpose of This Document**


This document serves as a **centralized reference** for developers using the Shopee Open Platform Chatbot across different regions.


Shopee operates in multiple markets, and the use of Chatbot functionalities is subject to **region-specific Chatbot Terms of Service (ToS)**. These regional terms govern the access, usage, and compliance requirements applicable to developers in each market.



Developers are responsible for understanding and complying with the applicable regional Chatbot Terms of Service when using Shopee Open Platform Chatbot.
